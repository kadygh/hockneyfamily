The Hockney Family of Lincolnshire

Generation Two (Children of Ralph Hockney and Mary Graves)

(3) Richard Hockney (Ralph1) was born in 1700 in Clee as the son of Ralph Hockney and Mary Graves. He married Mary Smith on 8th February 1723 in Healing, Lincolnshire.[ 1 ] They had the following children:

  • (12) Mary Ann Hockney (1726 - 1738)[ 2 ][ 3 ] Died in childhood.
  • (13) + John Hockney (c. 1730 - 1811)[ a ] Married Dorothy Hankin in 1755, had issue.[ 3 ] Worked as a manservant in Tetney before marriage. Described as a "carrier" in burial record. Died in Tetney in 1811.
  • (14) Ann Hockney (1731 - 1738)[ 4 ][ 5 ] Died in childhood.
  • (15) Frances Hockney (1733 - ?)[ 6 ] Presumably died in childhood.
  • (16) + Richard Hockney (1736 - 1780)[ 7 ] Married Mary Lillyman in Gainsborough, had issue. A "militia man" in his marriage record. Died as a labourer at 43.
  • (17) Thomas Hockney (1738 - 1738)[ 8 ][ 9 ] Died in childhood.
  • (18) + Elizabeth Hockney (1743 - 1782)[ 10 ] Married William West in Gainsborough in 1769, had issue. Died in Tetney in 1782.

    Richard Hockney worked as a blacksmith to support his family, but eventually passed away in 1754, leaving his wife Mary a widower.[ 11 ]

    (8) Thomas Hockney (Ralph1) was baptised on 12th October 1710 in Clee, Lincolnshire as the son of Ralph Hockney and Mary Graves.[ 12 ] He married Mary Rashdell on 11th May 1738 in Healing, Lincolnshire at 28 years old.[ 13 ] They had the following children:

  • (19) + Martha Hockney (1739 - 1808)[ 14 ] Married John Taylor in Nettleton in 1766, had issue. Died in Gainsborough in Lincolnshire, 1808.
  • (20) + Hannah Hockney (1742 - 1806)[ 15 ] Married John Basnip in Waithe in 1767, had issue. Died in Waithe in Lincolnshire in 1806.
  • (21) + Thomas Hockney (1752 - 1837)[ 16 ] Married Mary Barnet in Elsham in 1780, had issue. Died in Elsham in Lincolnshire 1837.
  • (22) + Mary Hockney (1756 - ?)[ 17 ] Married George Sanderson in Clee in 1785, had issue.

    Thomas worked as a labourer[ 16 ], but died a pauper in 1775 in Healing. His wife Mary died in 1779 in Elsham, Lincolnshire.[ 18 ]

    (10) Rachael Hockney (Ralph1) was baptised on 20th March 1714 in Clee, Lincolnshire.[ 19 ] She married Christopher Gulson on 16th May 1745 in Grainsby, Lincolnshire.[ 20 ] They had the following children:

  • (25) John Gulson (1745 - ?)[ 23 ]
  • (23) Christopher Gulson (1747 - 1747)[ 21 ] Died in childhood.
  • (24) Ralph Gulson (1749 - ?)<[ 22 ]
  • (26) Samuel Gulson (1754 - 1754)[ 24 ]

    This family disappears after the baptism of their final child, Samuel, in 1754. Perhaps they migrated out of Britain? It is unknown, but no records can be found after 1754.
