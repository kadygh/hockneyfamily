The Hockney Family of Lincolnshire

Generation Three (Grandchildren of Ralph Hockney and Mary Graves)

(13) John Hockney (c. 1730 - 1811) (Richard2, Ralph1) was probably born around 1730 in Tetney, Lincolnshire as the son of Richard Hockney and Mary Smith. He married Dorothy Hankin on 13th May 1755 in Middle Rasen, Lincolnshire.[ 1 ] They had the following children:

  • (27) + Thomas Hockney (1756 - 1836)[ 2 ] Married Elizabeth Ashby in Tetney in 1779, had issue. Died in Laceby in 1836.
  • (28) Richard Hockney (1759 - 1816)[ 3 ] Married Mary Gunnell in Laceby in 1803, had no issue.[ 4 ] Died in Tetney in 1816.[ 5 ]
  • (29) John Hockney (1761 - 1761)[ 6 ][ 7 ] Died in infancy.
  • (30) Sarah Hockney (1762 - 1788)[ 8 ][ 9 ] Had no issue.
  • (31) + Elizabeth Hockney (1765 - ?)[ 10 ] Married John Simpson in Hatcliffe in 1791, possibly moved to Croft afterwards.
  • (32) + Mary Hockney (1768 - 1851)[ 11 ][ 12 ] Married John Thorn in Tetney in 1800.
  • (33) John Hockney (1771 - 1771)[ 13 ][ 14 ] Died in infancy.
  • (34) William Hockney (1772 - 1863)[ 15 ] Married Elizabeth Blythe in 1809 in Tetney. Died in Brigsley in 1861.

    • (16) Richard Hockney Jr. (1736 - 1780) (Richard2, Ralph1) was born in 1736 in Tetney, Lincolnshire as the son of Richard Hockney and Mary Smith.
