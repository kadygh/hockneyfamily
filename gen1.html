The Hockney Family of Lincolnshire

(1) Ralph Hockney was born around 1676. His place of birth is unknown, but some people speculate either Lincolnshire or Cheshire. He married Mary Graves on 30th January 1696.[ 1 ] They had the following children:

  • (2) Unknown Hockney (1697 - ?)[ 2 ], presumed to have died in infancy. One FamilySearch tree lists him as Thomas Hockney but with no sources.[ 3 ]
  • (3) + Richard Hockney (1700 - 1754) [ a ]. Married Mary Smith, had issue. Worked as a blacksmith, died in late 1754.
  • (4) William Hockney (1704 - 1781)[ 4 ][ 5 ] Possibly married Ann Robinson[ 6 ], had no issue. Worked as a labourer, died in 1781.
  • (5) Joseph Hockney (1706 - ?)[ 7 ], presumed to have died in childhood or died unmarried. FamilySearch says he died at 15 years old, but with no source.[ 8 ]
  • (6) Mary Hockney (1707 - ?)[ 9 ], likely to have died in infancy or childhood since her sister is named Mary too.
  • (7) Mary Hockney (1709 - ?)[ 10 ]. Might have had an illegitimate child in the 1740s.[ 11 ]
  • (8)+ Thomas Hockney (1710 - 1775)[ 12 ]. Married Mary Rashdell[ 13 ], had issue. Worked as a labourer. Described as a "pauper" in his burial record. Died 1775.
  • (9) Elizabeth Hockney (1713 - ?)[ 14 ]. Died in childhood, one FamilySearch tree says she died at 9, but without a source.[ 15 ]
  • (10)+ Rachael Hockney (1714 - ?)[ 16 ]. Married Christopher Gulson in Grainsby, Lincolnshire, 3 miles away from Tetney.[ 17 ]
  • (11) Sarah Hockney (1719 - ?)[ 18 ].

    1. ^ No baptism record has been located thus far for Richard, but through indirect sources I have attributed him to this family. In 1700, there were two Hockney families in Tetney: Ralph Hockney, and his wife Mary Graves, and William Hockney, and his wife Elizabeth Petch. These two were possibly brothers, and they both had a son in 1700. Ralph had Richard in 1700 and William had Samuel in 1700, therefore Richard could not be the son of William. The only person his father could be is Ralph. Also, through DNA matches, I have a confirmed DNA match at 9 cM who is related to me through Thomas Hockney, a son of Ralph Hockney.
